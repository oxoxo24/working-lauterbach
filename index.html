Three ways to work together

The purpose comes first.

Clear expectations make stronger work. Before a date is confirmed, we decide which of the three formats applies.

They are deliberately different. A free project is not an unpaid portfolio commission, and a paid model booking is not a shared production with competing goals.

One rule for every format

Unless a written limitation is agreed before the shoot, I may select, edit and use photographs from every session for my portfolio, website, social media, publications, exhibitions and other artistic, editorial or self-promotional purposes.
